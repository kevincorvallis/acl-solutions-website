SmartSpender Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

ACL Solutions LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the SmartSpender web application and mobile application (collectively, the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use SmartSpender.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Authentication tokens from third-party providers (Google Sign-In)

Financial Data You Upload

When you upload bank or credit card statements, we process:

  • Transaction dates, amounts, and descriptions
  • Merchant names extracted from transaction descriptions
  • Category assignments (automatically generated)

This data is stored securely and associated with your user account. You can delete your data at any time.

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Questions and queries you submit to the AI assistant
  • App features accessed and interaction patterns
  • Device type and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide personalized financial analysis and insights
  • Categorize transactions and track spending patterns
  • Generate AI-powered recommendations and answers to your questions
  • Calculate budgets and spending trends
  • Improve App performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • AWS Infrastructure: Data is stored on Amazon Web Services with enterprise-grade security
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls ensure only authorized systems access your data
  • Data Isolation: Each user's data is isolated and accessible only to that user

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Cloud infrastructure including Lambda, DynamoDB, and S3. See AWS Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Bedrock (Claude AI): Powers the AI assistant for natural language processing. Queries are processed but not used to train AI models. See Bedrock FAQs.
  • Vercel: Hosts the web application frontend. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication provider. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your financial information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers who help operate the App (as described above)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or others

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Transaction Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Query History: Retained for 90 days to enable query history features
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access: View all your transaction data and query history in the App
  • Delete: Delete individual transactions or all your data
  • Export: Export your data in standard formats
  • Account Deletion: Request complete account deletion by contacting support

8. Children's Privacy

SmartSpender is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, through the App or email.
